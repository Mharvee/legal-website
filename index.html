Who We Are

A Legacy of Legal
Excellence

Emeka Onohwakpor & Co. (Thompson Chambers) is a firm of young, experienced, multi-talented and vibrant attorneys who take pride in their reputation for excellence, responsiveness and uncompromised professionalism.

Our head office is in Lagos with additional offices in Port Harcourt, Abuja, Benin City, Warri, Sapele and Onitsha. We represent our clients throughout Nigeria and internationally through our overseas partners.
